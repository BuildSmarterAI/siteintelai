## 1. Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goal
Optimize the homepage to increase user engagement, drive conversions, and clearly communicate the value proposition of BuildSmarter™ Feasibility.

### Key Objectives
- **Improve Clarity**: Ensure the homepage clearly articulates what BuildSmarter™ Feasibility offers.
- **Increase Engagement**: Encourage users to explore the platform's features and benefits.
- **Drive Conversions**: Guide users towards starting a free trial or requesting a demo.
- **Enhance Credibility**: Build trust by showcasing data accuracy, lender-ready reports, and customer testimonials.
- **Optimize for Mobile**: Ensure a seamless experience across all devices.

## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Audience
Commercial real estate developers, lenders, and brokers seeking rapid feasibility assessments.

### User Personas
- **Developer**: Needs quick insights to evaluate potential development sites.
- **Lender**: Requires accurate data for underwriting and risk assessment.
- **Broker**: Wants to provide clients with comprehensive property analyses.

## 3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Engagement Metrics
- **Bounce Rate**: Percentage of visitors who leave the site after viewing only one page.
- **Time on Page**: Average duration visitors spend on the homepage.
- **Scroll Depth**: Percentage of visitors who scroll to specific sections of the page.
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Percentage of visitors who click on key CTAs.

### Conversion Metrics
- **Trial Sign-ups**: Number of users who start a free trial.
- **Demo Requests**: Number of users who request a product demo.
- **Contact Form Submissions**: Number of users who submit the contact form.
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of visitors who complete a desired action (e.g., sign-up, demo request).

### Performance Benchmarks
- **Target Bounce Rate**: < 40%
- **Target Time on Page**: > 2 minutes
- **Target Conversion Rate**: > 5%

## 4. Core Sections & Content Elements

### 4.1 Hero Section
- **Headline**: Compelling value proposition (e.g., "Instant Feasibility Reports for Commercial Real Estate").
- **Subheadline**: Brief explanation of the platform's benefits (e.g., "Data-driven insights in 60 seconds").
- **Visual**: High-quality image or video showcasing the platform's interface or a successful project.
- **Call-to-Action (CTA)**: Primary CTA (e.g., "Start Free Trial," "Request a Demo").
- **Optional**: Supporting elements like trust badges or social proof.

### 4.2 Problem/Solution Section
- **Problem Statement**: Clearly define the challenges faced by the target audience (e.g., "Traditional feasibility studies take weeks and cost thousands").
- **Solution Statement**: Explain how BuildSmarter™ Feasibility solves these problems (e.g., "Get lender-ready reports in minutes, at a fraction of the cost").
- **Visual**: Illustrative graphics or icons representing the problem and solution.

### 4.3 Features & Benefits Section
- **Key Features**: Highlight the platform's core capabilities (e.g., "Automated Data Collection," "AI-Powered Analysis," "Lender-Ready Reports").
- **Benefits**: Explain the advantages of using these features (e.g., "Save Time," "Reduce Costs," "Increase Accuracy").
- **Visuals**: Accompanying icons or screenshots to illustrate each feature.

### 4.4 How It Works Section
- **Step-by-Step Guide**: Simple explanation of the platform's workflow (e.g., "Enter Property Address," "Generate Report," "Download PDF").
- **Visuals**: Flowchart or animated graphics to guide users through the process.

### 4.5 Social Proof Section
- **Testimonials**: Quotes from satisfied customers highlighting the platform's value.
- **Case Studies**: Brief summaries of successful projects or use cases.
- **Logos**: Display logos of well-known clients or partners.

### 4.6 Pricing Section
- **Pricing Plans**: Clear presentation of available pricing options and features.
- **Value Proposition**: Emphasize the cost savings and ROI compared to traditional methods.
- **CTA**: Encourage users to select a plan or contact sales.

### 4.7 Call-to-Action (CTA) Section
- **Primary CTA**: Prominent button encouraging users to start a free trial or request a demo.
- **Secondary CTAs**: Options for users who need more information (e.g., "Learn More," "Contact Us").

### 4.8 Footer Section
- **Navigation Links**: Links to key pages (e.g., "About Us," "Contact," "Privacy Policy").
- **Contact Information**: Email address, phone number, and physical address (if applicable).
- **Social Media Links**: Links to the company's social media profiles.
- **Copyright Notice**: Copyright information and legal disclaimers.

## 7. Mobile Responsiveness

### 7.1 Breakpoints
- **Desktop**: > 1200px
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1200px
- **Mobile**: < 768px

### 7.2 Layout Adjustments
- **Navigation**: Collapsed menu on mobile devices.
- **Content**: Stacked content sections for easy scrolling.
- **Imagery**: Optimized images for mobile devices.
- **Buttons**: Full-width buttons for easy tapping.

## 8. Accessibility

### 8.1 WCAG Compliance
- **Adherence**: WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
- **Color Contrast**: Ensure sufficient color contrast for text and background elements.
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Ensure all elements are accessible via keyboard navigation.
- **Screen Reader Compatibility**: Provide alternative text for images and ensure proper ARIA attributes.

### 8.2 Specific Considerations
- **Semantic HTML**: Use semantic HTML elements to improve accessibility.
- **Form Labels**: Provide clear and concise labels for all form fields.
- **Error Messages**: Provide clear and helpful error messages for form validation.
